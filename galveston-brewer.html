At-Home Recommendations
Three development areas to focus on between sessions
🔀 Agility & Quickness
1
Set up 4–5 cones and practice a T-drill or figure-8 pattern 3x per week. Time yourself and try to beat it.
2
Practice defensive shuffles and sharp 180° direction changes — low, quick cuts beat big looping turns every time.
⚽️ Technical Ball Work
1
Spend 10–15 minutes daily juggling, doing wall passes, and first-touch exercises — comfort on the ball compounds over time with consistent reps.
2
Practice receiving with both feet on multiple surfaces. The more natural it feels, the faster you'll execute when it matters in a game.
⚡ Speed & Explosiveness
1
Short sprint bursts of 10–20 yards, 2–3x per week. Focus on your first two steps — that's where games are won and lost.
2
Run 30–40 yard strides at full effort: relaxed shoulders, high knee drive, full extension behind you.
